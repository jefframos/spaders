How To Play

  • Use your Spader arrows to attack the Spaders on the board
  • Your life is the amount of damage you deal.

  • Dealing enough damage will wipe the Spader from the board.
  • Your objective is to wipe all spaders with a colored tile behind
  • Attack another spader that have an arrow to counter attack, so both get damaged
